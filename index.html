🧠 Memory Match – Test Your Brain!

🧠 Welcome to Memory Match! Test your focus and short-term memory in this fast-paced card-flipping challenge. Flip two cards — if they match, they stay open; if not, they flip back. Match all pairs in the fewest moves to win! Think you're sharp? Let's find out!

📋 How to Play

  • Flip your first card.
  • Click any two cards and reveal what’s inside.
  • If the two cards match, they stay face-up.
  • Continue flipping until all card pairs are matched.
  • 🎉 Find all pairs to win and celebrate your memory skills!

Rock Paper Scissors – AI Edition

🪨📄✂ Rock, Paper, Scissors – Digital Edition! Challenge the AI in a quick, fun battle of luck and logic. Pick your move and see the AI’s response instantly. Scores update automatically — can you outsmart the machine? Ready to play?

📋 How to Play

  • Click on one of the three choices: ✊🏻 📄 ✂ .
  • The AI will automatically select its move in real-time.
  • The result will be shown instantly.
  • Your score and the AI’s score are displayed side by side for every round.

🎮 Tic Tac Toe – Classic 1v1 Fun!

Play a fun 1v1 game offline on the same device. Player 1 is ❌ and Player 2 is ⭕. X always starts. Take turns placing symbols — get three in a row to win! No winner? It’s a draw. Hit 🔁 to play again anytime!

📋 How to Play

  • The game starts automatically when the first player clicks a box.
  • Player 1 uses ❌ and Player 2 uses ⭕.
  • Click on an empty square to place your symbol.
  • Alternate turns until one player gets three in a row or the game ends in a draw.
  • Use the 🔁 Reset button to play again anytime.

🐍Snake Game🐍

The classic Snake Game is a timeless favorite! Control the hungry snake as it moves around the board, collecting food to grow longer. But be careful — don’t crash into the walls or your own tail, or it's game over! This version is built using pure JavaScript, CSS, and HTML, giving a smooth and nostalgic experience. It's a test of your reflexes, timing, and strategy. How long can you survive?

📋 How to Play ?

  • Use arrow keys to move the snake (↑ ↓ ← →)
  • Eat food to grow and score points
  • Avoid walls and your own tail — or it’s game over
  • The snake gets faster, so stay sharp and beat your high score!

BRICK GAME

Brick Game is a modern take on the classic arcade brick breaker. Your mission is simple: use the paddle to bounce the ball and destroy all the bricks on the screen. But there's a twist — you have 10 handcrafted levels to conquer. Each level is harder, faster, and more intense than the last!

🕹 How to Play

  • Move your paddle left/right to bounce the ball
  • Break all the bricks to complete the level
  • Advance automatically to the next level
  • Don’t lose all your chances — the game ends if you do
  • Finish Level 10 to win the entire game!
